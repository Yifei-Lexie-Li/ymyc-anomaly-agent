# Content Performance Metrics

## Completion Rate

Completion rate measures the share of content interactions that reach the
defined completion threshold. A decline may indicate weak content relevance,
excessive content length, poor audience targeting, or a mismatch between the
content promise and the actual experience.

Completion rate should be reviewed by user segment, content topic, content
format, and distribution channel. A segment-level decline is an investigation
signal, not proof of a specific root cause.

## Average Watch Time

Average watch time measures how long users spend consuming content. A decline
can indicate weaker early engagement, content pacing problems, or distribution
to users with low intent.

## Active User Retention

Active user retention measures the percentage of users active in one period who
remain active in the next period. When retention declines, compare user tiers,
content engagement, visit frequency, and changes in the content mix.
