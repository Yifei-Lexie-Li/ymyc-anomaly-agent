# Content Performance Playbook

## Deep-Dive Content

Deep-dive content may lose completion when the introduction is too long, the
structure is difficult to scan, or users cannot identify the main conclusion
quickly. Broad distribution to low-intent audiences can also reduce completion.

Recommended checks:

- Compare content length and completion by section.
- Inspect where users stop reading or watching.
- Review whether the title accurately reflects the main analysis.
- Move key conclusions and charts closer to the beginning.
- Add summaries, headings, and section navigation.

## Macro Topics

When one macro topic underperforms, review whether the topic is timely, whether
the angle is differentiated, and whether the content matches the audience's
investment interests. Topic-level performance can also be affected by an
unusual mix of formats or distribution channels.
