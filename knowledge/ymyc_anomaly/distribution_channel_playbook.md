# Distribution Channel Playbook

## Newsletter

When newsletter-driven completion declines, investigate the relationship
between the email promise and the destination content. A strong subject line
can generate interest while the landing content fails to meet expectations.

Recommended checks:

- Compare subject line, preview text, and destination content.
- Review audience targeting and send frequency.
- Check mobile readability and landing-page speed.
- Compare newsletter traffic with dashboard and economics-home traffic.
- Review whether long-form content is appropriate for the email audience.

Possible actions:

- Tighten subject-line and content alignment.
- Send deep-dive content to higher-intent audience segments.
- Use a concise summary before linking to the full analysis.
